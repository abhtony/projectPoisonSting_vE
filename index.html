How to Help When Your Child Gets a Needle

Why does my child need a needle poke

In hospitals needle pokes can be used for:

Bloodwork

IV Medications & Fluids

Numbing Injections for Stitching

The difference between bloodwork (venipuncture) and IVs

Needle poke to take few drops of blood

Needle poke to insert straw: needle comes out, but straw stays in

Why does my child need an IV?

Some medicines cannot be swallowed. They need to be given directly into the blood.

Sometimes medicines given by mouth just isn't strong enough.

Sometimes the body needs an extra drink of fluids directly into the blood to fix dehydration.

Sometimes we need a tiny blood sample for learning what we need to do.

Tips for everyone

1. Be calm and use encouraging language

Be a role model for your child - they are looking to you to understand how they should feel.

Donâ€™t say sorry, but tell them how great they are doing!

2. Try numbing cream

Numbing cream can be used for all ages.

But remember they require 30 - 60 minutes to work!

3. Ask for help

You can always ask health care professionals for help!

Tips for Babies

Distractions

Give sugar water 1-2 minutes beforethe procedure.

Start breastfeeding your infant immediately before the procedure and continue during and after.

Distract your infant at the time of the poke. (Bells, toys, etc.)

Comfort Holds

Helps recover faster

Tips for Children

Distractions

Give an alternative to focusing on pain that is more positive and fun!

Best if child is involved in choosing the form of distraction.

Here are some suggestions:

Video Games

Singing

Blow Bubbles

Comfort Holds

The Joey Position

Caregiver sits on bed or chair - child sits on lap facing outwsard

Child's leg can be tucked in between caregiver's leg as needed

The Bear Hug

Caregiver sits on bed or chair - child straddles chest to chest

Child's legs are positioned so that they are stable

Tips for Teens

Distractions & Comforts

Let your teen decide what they need and what will work best for them.

Give open and honest answers to any questions they may have.

Suggest that they take a deep breathe and look away.

Distraction helps - maybe bring your favourite tunes!

Also remember!

Positive attitude and support can go a long way. Everyone needs a confidence boost sometimes!
